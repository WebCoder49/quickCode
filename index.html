Private. Quick. Easy. This is quickCode.

quickCode runs right in your browser and lets you edit, run and save code files quickly and easily! There's no need to provide any personal information or sign in, and quickCode doesn't use cookies!

Policy

By using quickCode, you agree to the following:

Privacy

The main aim of quickCode is to stay 100% private, while being as functional, quick and easy as possible. You don't even need to sign in! Rather than using cookies, which don't have the best reputation, quickCode stores your files and preferences locally in your browser on your computer and does not send them to the server. Therefore, sharing links and files cannot be recovered as I (WebCoder49) do not even know what they are, as quickCode is so private!
